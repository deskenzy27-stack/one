Frequently Asked Questions

What are your application requirements?

We require a completed application, proof of income (3x monthly rent), government-issued ID, and a credit check. Application fee is $50 per applicant.

Do you allow pets?

Yes, we are a pet-friendly community! We allow up to 2 pets per apartment with a maximum weight of 50 lbs each. There is a one-time pet fee of $300 and monthly pet rent of $35 per pet.

What utilities are included in the rent?

Water, sewer, and trash are included in your monthly rent. Residents are responsible for electricity, gas, internet, and cable services.

Is parking available?

Yes, we offer underground garage parking. Reserved parking spaces are available for $150 per month. Electric vehicle charging stations are also available.

What is your lease term?

Our standard lease term is 12 months. We also offer flexible lease options from 6-18 months, with adjusted pricing for shorter terms.
